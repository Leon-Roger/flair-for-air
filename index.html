Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our AC services and maintenance.

How often should I service my air conditioner?

We recommend servicing your AC unit at least once a year, ideally before the start of the cooling season. For heavily used systems or in dusty environments, twice-yearly maintenance may be beneficial to ensure optimal performance and efficiency.

What's included in a general AC service?

Our comprehensive general service includes: cleaning or replacing air filters, checking and cleaning condenser and evaporator coils, inspecting and cleaning drain lines, checking refrigerant levels, testing thermostat operation, inspecting electrical connections, and lubricating moving parts.

How long does an AC service typically take?

A standard service appointment usually takes between 45 minutes to 2 hours, depending on the type and condition of your AC unit. More complex systems or those requiring additional repairs may take longer.

What are signs that my AC needs repair?

Common warning signs include: reduced cooling capacity, unusual noises, foul odors, frequent cycling on and off, higher than normal energy bills, water leaks, or the system not turning on at all. If you notice any of these issues, it's best to call for service promptly.

Do you offer emergency services?

Yes, we provide 24/7 emergency AC repair services for situations where your cooling system has completely failed, especially during extreme weather conditions. Emergency service calls are prioritized and we strive to respond as quickly as possible.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, all major credit/debit cards, UPI payments, and bank transfers. For larger projects like installations, we also offer financing options through partner providers.
